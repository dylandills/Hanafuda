How To Play:


**Basic Rules**

  • The game uses a deck of 48 cards.
  • Game is played with two players.
  • Eight cards are dealt face-down to each player.
  • The player with the earliest birthday month is the dealer.(This simply determines who goes first.)
  • Each round, a player picks a card from the deck.
  • The player takes the card that was dealt and tries to match a card to a card on the playing field. If there is a matching suit on the playing field, the player takes the card(s). For example, "Matsu" would match a "Poetry Ribbon" in the January suite. The player would keep these cards.
  • In the case that there isn't a match, the card is expelled to the center of the table.
  • If a player is dealt 4 pairs OR 2 suits, the player automatically wins the round.
